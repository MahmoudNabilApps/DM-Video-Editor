Privacy Policy – DM Video Editor

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Summary

This app is fully offline for editing and export. We do not collect, transmit, or store your videos, audio, or projects on our servers. All processing happens on your device.

Permissions

  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO / storage: To let you import videos from your gallery for editing.
  • RECORD_AUDIO: Only if you use voiceover or audio features that capture microphone input. Audio is saved locally as part of your project.

Data we do not collect

We do not require an account. We do not track you for advertising. We do not sell data. There are no third-party analytics SDKs processing your media in this policy scope.

Exported files

When you export, files are written to your device (e.g. Movies folder) using Android’s media store. Only you and apps you grant access to can read those files.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the app store listing.

The same text is available at the HTTPS URL configured in the app (Google Play → App content → Privacy policy).
